At Bauge, a construction company, I worked as an Architect with the following responsibilities:

- **Documentation Adjustment**: Adjusting construction documentation to ensure accuracy and compliance with project requirements.
- **Site Assistance**: Assisting site supervisors and project directors in managing construction activities and resolving on-site issues.
- **Major Project Participation**: Participating in a large-scale airport project in Esquel, contributing to its successful planning and execution.
- **Technical Solutions**: Proposing technical solutions to address challenges and ensure the effective materialization of construction projects.

My role at Bauge involved a combination of technical expertise and practical problem-solving to support the successful completion of various construction projects.
